Valabhipur Plates of Dhruvasena I, [Valabhī] year 226, Kārttika śu. 15

Metadata

Current Version: 

draft, 2022-07-21Z

Editors:  

Annette Schmiedchen

Identifier:

INSMaitraka00023

Summary:

Grant of land (probably 100 pādāvarta) in favour of a Brāhmaṇa, his name, the details of his Vedic affiliation, etc. being lost.
